== About BlackWhite Lite ==

Be the leader in your industry by taking your online publishing to the next level with Black-White Wordpress Magazine Theme. Easily let your readers go through content by category, archive with clean and well-organized homepage magazine layout. 

== Installation ==
	
1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Type BlackWhite Lite in search box. Click Install Now on the theme.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
